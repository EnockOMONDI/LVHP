BIO

Intro.

Growing up listening to radio shows like ( Hits not homework and Dance Republic) on capital fm made me to fallin love with music,none of my friends could pin me down Musically.This inspired me to want to become one of the top entertainers in kenya.It was only a few years later I started following my favorite Djs every weekend till I learned how to Dj. Now a regular fixture of Nairobi's nightlife and event Scene Dj Seanizell brand has shared stage with well-known djs and landed a regular gig at Dance republic, a capital fm show which airs every friday 0900hrs, the same show he used to listen to while growing up.
